Quick Answers

How quickly will I hear back?

I typically respond to inquiries within 1-2 business days. If you're reaching out about becoming a client, I'll send you information about next steps and scheduling a discovery call.

I'm not in Kentucky. Can I still work with you?

Clinical NP services are only available to Kentucky residents. However, non-clinical coaching and educational services (like courses and group programs) are available to anyone.

How do I book a free consultation?

You can apply to work together through the client portal, and I'll reach out to schedule a free 20-minute discovery call to discuss your goals and see if we're a good fit.
