About Alicia

My Story

I'm Alicia—a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner, wellness mentor, and mom. My passion for holistic health began watching my grandfather manage chronic conditions through dietary and lifestyle interventions.

After years in critical care nursing, I experienced my own health struggles and discovered how deeply our hormones are impacted by metabolism, stress, nutrition, and daily habits.

Now, I help women like you feel supported, educated, and empowered—so healthy habits actually stick, without the overwhelm.
