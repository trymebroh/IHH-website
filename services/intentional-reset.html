The Problem Isn't Protocols.
It's Doing Them Blind.

Most women jump straight to supplements, diets, or wellness trends - without ever understanding why they feel the way they do.

The Intentional Health Reset takes a different approach. Before we create any plan, we assess:

  • How you're feeling (symptoms, energy, mood)
  • How you're living (stress, sleep, nutrition)
  • What your labs actually show
  • Your mineral patterns and stress physiology
  • What's realistic for your life right now

Only then do we build a protocol - one that's intentional, appropriate, and supportive instead of overwhelming.
