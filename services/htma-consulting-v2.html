About HTMA

What is Hair Tissue Mineral Analysis?

HTMA is a screening test that measures mineral content in your hair. It provides a blueprint of your body's mineral status over the past 3-4 months, revealing patterns that blood tests often miss.

This non-invasive test helps identify mineral imbalances, heavy metal exposure, metabolic patterns, and stress responses that may be affecting your hormones, energy, and overall health.

Unlike blood tests that show a snapshot in time, HTMA reveals long-term patterns and trends—making it an invaluable tool for understanding the root causes of chronic symptoms.

My services are designed for women in their childbearing years—whether you're preparing for pregnancy, currently pregnant, postpartum, or simply wanting to optimize your hormone health. One-off lab reviews are available for others outside this demographic.

Do I need to be a Kentucky resident?

Clinical NP services are only available to Kentucky residents who are physically located in Kentucky during telehealth appointments. However, non-clinical coaching and educational services are available to anyone.

HTMA is always paired with coaching and interpretation—it's not offered as a standalone test. This ensures you receive proper context and guidance for your results.
