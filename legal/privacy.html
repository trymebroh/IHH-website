Privacy Policy

Intention Holistic Health PLLC
Last Updated: November 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Intention Holistic Health PLLC ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, protects, and discloses your information when you visit www.intentionholistichealth.com ("Website") or engage with our services.

By using the Website or providing information to us, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

1. Company Information

Intention Holistic Health PLLC is a Texas-registered Professional Limited Liability Company (PLLC).

Clinical services are performed by Alicia Harrison, MSN, APRN, FNP-C, licensed in the Commonwealth of Kentucky and state of Texas.

Business operations follow Texas law; clinical care follows Kentucky law and telehealth regulations. Clinical care is not provided in Texas.

2. Information We Collect

We collect two categories of information:

  • Non-Personal and Personal Information (Website use)
  • Protected Health Information (PHI) (clinical services only)

2.1 Personal Information (Website or Coaching Services)

When you visit the Website, subscribe to content, or engage in non-clinical coaching services, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Postal address
  • Date of birth (if provided voluntarily)
  • Any information you choose to submit through website forms
  • Payment information processed through secure third-party providers
  • Preferences or interests you share with us

This information is considered general personal information, not PHI, and is not subject to HIPAA.

2.2 Usage Information (Automatically Collected)

We automatically collect information such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Operating system
  • Referring website
  • Pages viewed and actions taken
  • Date and time of visits

This helps us understand Website usage and improve user experience.

2.3 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We may use:

  • Cookies
  • Web beacons
  • Pixels
  • Analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics)

These help improve the Website and understand how visitors interact with it.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some Website features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

PHI is collected only when you become a clinical patient of Intention Holistic Health PLLC.

PHI is collected solely through:

  • Practice Better (HIPAA-compliant client portal)
  • HIPAA-compliant telehealth platforms
  • Clinical intake forms and consent documents

  • Website forms
  • Email

Clinical patients receive a separate HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices (NPP).

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 To Provide Coaching or Website Services (Non-PHI)

We may use personal information to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Provide non-clinical coaching services
  • Deliver digital products
  • Send newsletters or marketing emails
  • Improve the Website, services, and user experience
  • Perform administrative and operational functions

3.2 To Provide Clinical Services (PHI)

PHI is used only to:

  • Provide clinical nurse practitioner services
  • Conduct telehealth visits
  • Document care
  • Process labs or referrals (as authorized by you)
  • Comply with applicable healthcare regulations

We do not sell PHI or use it for marketing.

4. Disclosure of Information

We may disclose information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share non-PHI personal information with vendors who assist with:

  • Website hosting
  • Email delivery
  • Payment processing
  • Marketing automation
  • Administrative services

These providers only receive the information necessary to perform their functions.

4.2 HIPAA-Compliant Clinical Services

PHI may be shared with:

  • HIPAA-compliant telehealth platforms
  • Labs (if authorized by you)
  • Healthcare entities involved in your care

PHI is not shared outside secure and compliant systems.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to:

  • Comply with laws and regulations
  • Respond to subpoenas or lawful requests
  • Protect our legal rights

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical, administrative, and physical measures to secure your information.

However, no electronic system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

You acknowledge:

  • Email, Kajabi, and website forms are not HIPAA-secure
  • PHI should only be shared via Practice Better

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed toward individuals under age 18.

If we learn we have collected personal information from a minor without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights (Clinical Patients)

If you become a clinical patient, you have rights under HIPAA, including:

  • Access to your records
  • Requesting corrections
  • Requesting restrictions on disclosures
  • Requesting confidential communications

These rights are described in your HIPAA NPP.

8. Your Rights (General Website Users)

You may:

  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Request access to or deletion of your personal information (non-PHI)
  • Update your contact information

Contact us at the email below for assistance.
