### 5. WHAT'S INCLUDED - 5 STEPS
**Background:** Image background (#dacec1)

**Step 1: Comprehensive Intake Questionnaire**
Before appointment, complete in-depth intake form covering symptoms, history, lifestyle, medications, supplements, and goals.

**Step 2: Upload of Your Most Recent Labs**
Upload labs for review (bloodwork, thyroid panels, hormones, metabolic labs, lipid panels, etc.)

**Step 3: 40-Minute Functional Lab Review Call**
Walk through lab results line-by-line using functional ranges and whole-body context:
- What each marker means
- What trends or patterns are emerging
- How labs relate to symptoms
- Which areas need attention

**Step 4: Personalized Recommendations**
Summary that may include:
- Nutrition guidance
- Lifestyle recommendations
- Supplement considerations (optional)
- Suggestions for follow-up labs
- Clear next steps

**Step 5: Optional Next-Step Pathways**

- Continued à-la-carte lab review consults
