My Story

My Pappy - where it all began

My passion for holistic health started early. I grew up watching my Pappy manage his Parkinson's symptoms and type 2 diabetes through diet and lifestyle. That planted a seed that eventually led me to nursing—and later, functional health.

After earning my Bachelor's in Nursing, I worked in critical care and felt frustrated seeing so many preventable conditions—while facing my own issues with fatigue, breakouts, and poor sleep. Through my own healing, I discovered how much our hormones are impacted by metabolism, stress, nutrition, and daily habits.

I went on to earn my Master of Science in Nursing to become a Family Nurse Practitioner, and completed advanced training in nutrition and HTMA interpretation to help women thrive through preconception, pregnancy, and postpartum.
